Place contains all data used in thesis.

The data is extracted from a finance company's unsecured personal loan using SAS Enterprise Guide software.

Rating, Tenor, Principle amount, Interest Rate probability and Rejected Data are from bookings in January 2025. Other data comes from bookings between January 1st 2022 to September 30 2024.

rating_prob.csv is internal application scorecard.
rating_prob2.csv is CIC score.

out_of_sample_dataset.csv if random loans disbursed between January 1st 2022 and September 30 2024.
out_of_sample_opt.csv is data after running simulation.
